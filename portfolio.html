Overview

Reimagining how diners discover food

Traditional menus rely on flat photos that rarely match reality. RELISH replaces them with interactive 3D models diners can rotate, zoom, and drop onto their table using AR — bridging the gap between expectation and plate.

The Problem

Diners order blind. Static photos don't convey portion size, texture, or how a dish actually looks. This leads to mismatched expectations, wasted food, and lower satisfaction.

The Solution

A web-based AR menu that lets customers interact with photorealistic 3D models of every dish before ordering — no app download required. Point your phone, place the dish on your table, and see exactly what you're getting.

My Role

End-to-end product design and development — from concept and 3D asset curation to UI design, WebGL rendering, AR integration, and deployment.

Timeline

Designed and built as a working prototype, ready for restaurant pilot testing. Fully functional on both Android and iOS devices.

Key Features

More than a menu

🔄

360° 3D Viewer

Every dish renders as a real-time 3D model. Drag to rotate, inspect from any angle — see the textures, layers, and details before you order.

📱

Native AR

Tap "View in your space" to place a life-sized dish on your actual table using your phone's camera. Works on Android (Scene Viewer) and iOS (Quick Look).

🛒

Full Ordering Flow

Browse by category, add items with pairings, manage quantities, and review your cart — a complete end-to-end ordering experience.

🎨

Crafted UI

Warm, tactile design system with Instrument Serif display type, smooth transitions, and a cream-and-ink palette that feels premium yet approachable.

⚡

No App Required

Pure web technology — runs in any mobile browser. Restaurants can deploy it with a QR code on the table. Zero friction for diners.

🔍

Dish Details

Calories, prep time, spice level, full ingredients list, allergen warnings, and real reviews — everything a diner needs at a glance.
